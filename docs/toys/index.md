## Scrollable Grid Demo

An interactive large-grid toy for placing colored blocks, panning around a big board, and zooming the workspace in and out.

- [Open the demo](scrollable-grid.html)
- Source origin: `C:\Users\Harsh\Downloads\CSE 583\learn_front_end\index.html`

### What It Does

- Lets you drag colored pieces onto a 100 by 100 board.
- Supports zooming, panning, a minimap, and quick jump-to-cell controls.
- Works well as a compact interaction prototype and UI sandbox.
